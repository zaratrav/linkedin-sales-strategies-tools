About Sales Navigator Strategies

Sales Navigator strategies focus on building scalable B2B prospecting systems using data, timing, and targeting precision.

SDR workflows combine lead discovery, qualification, and outreach into repeatable systems.

Trigger events such as job changes, company growth, and funding rounds create high-conversion opportunities.

Intent signals and alerts help identify warm prospects and improve outreach timing.

Persona targeting ensures you reach decision-makers, including C-suite executives and key stakeholders.

Combining workflows, targeting, and signals creates a powerful outbound sales engine.
